Digital Divide

Bridging gaps through digital literacy and inclusion

Introduction

Technology is essential in modern life, but not everyone has the skills to use it effectively. This creates a digital skills gap where people struggle to fully participate in digital activities.

Vision

To create an inclusive society where everyone has equal access to digital skills and technology.

Mission

To reduce the digital skills gap by providing training, tools, and learning resources.

Purpose

To help students improve their digital skills for school and future jobs.

Description

The digital skills gap is the difference between people who can use technology well and those who cannot.
