Digital Divide

Bridging gaps through digital literacy and inclusion

Introduction

Technology is essential in modern life, but not everyone has the skills to use it effectively. This creates a digital skills gap.

Vision

To create an inclusive society where everyone has equal access to digital skills.

Mission

Provide tools, training, and resources to reduce the digital skills gap.

Purpose

Help students improve their digital skills for academic success.

Description

The gap between people who can and cannot use technology effectively.
